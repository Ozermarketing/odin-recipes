Lasagna Recipe

Description

This easy Lasagna Recipe is meaty, cheesy, and packed with flavor in every bite. Homemade lasagna is better than any restaurant version and it feeds a crowd for way less than going out to eat.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Red wine
  • Marinara sauce
  • Dried thyme
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Egg

  1. Brown the beef
  2. Finish the sauce
  3. Make the cheese filling
  4. Prep
  5. Assemble
  6. Bake
  7. Rest
